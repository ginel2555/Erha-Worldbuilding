# Current Races of Erha

## Overview
Erha hosts several unique races, each with distinct characteristics and origins.

### Uk Man
- **Origin**: Vatra Continent
- **Physical Traits**: Pale-blue skin, muscular, black/brown hair
- **Cultural Characteristics**: Nomadic, spiritual connection to beasts

### Rain Man
- **Origin**: Other Continent refugees
- **Language**: Derived from Rimaunoa or Motherspeak

### Kel Man
- **Origin**: Other Continent refugees
- **Physical Traits**: Dark-skinned, gray-haired, white eyes
- **Special Ability**: Exceptionally skilled at nature magic

### Amaal
- **Type**: Human-Alien hybrid
- **Physical Traits**: Short, stocky, gray-blue skin
- **Social Structure**: Elaborate caste system
- **Notable Cities**: Gholain (Capital), Xaas, Jojek, Tlul, Baax, Niba

### Tyrian
- **Type**: Human-Seahorse hybrid
- **Origin**: Ocean natives
- **Unique Trait**: Cursed to walk on land forever
- **Government**: Thalassocracy

### Rotmen
- **Type**: Human-Mushroom hybrid
- **Origin**: Vatra continent
- **Created by**: An Othergod in ancient times

### Ulmer
- **Origin**: Original human species
- **Physical Traits**: Green-skinned, tall, no ears
- **Special Abilities**: Exceptional ice and sound magic
- **Current Status**: Few remain, living in mountain caves
